SKAKSHOUKA

Shakshuka is a hearty and delicious North African dish from Tunisia. It consists of eggs topped with a deliciously seasoned onion-tomato base. We love how it's easy to make with simple ingredients - and it's just one bed dish. Full of flavor and comfort for breakfast or any meal of the day. Serve with warm pita bread, chips or bread of your choice.

CREME BRULEE

Reme Brulee also known as burnt ice cream, crema catalana, or Trinity ice cream is a dessert consisting of a fatty custard base topped with a hard caramel sauce. It is usually used at room temperature. The custard base is traditionally flavored with vanilla, but can also come in a variety of flavors. When it comes to Creme Brulee, people often think of a blend of sweet and attractive smooth, with a layer of hard toasted sugar on top.

CURRY CAKE

Curry Cake also known as Japanese curry bread, is one of the most popular cakes in Japan. Cakes are used to make lunch or dinner, sometimes as dessert at a party. Learning how to be able to make this crispy, delicious and correct cake is what the Cooking Lessons section gives you in this post! Surely this cake will conquer you and your friends right from the first bite.
